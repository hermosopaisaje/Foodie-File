Longganisa

Isabela is known for its delicious longganisa, which is a type of Filipino sausage that is typically made with ground pork, garlic, and other seasonings. The longganisa of Isabela is especially popular because of its distinct flavor and texture. Isabela's longganisa is made with a combination of pork belly and ground pork, which gives it a unique flavor and texture. The meat is mixed with minced garlic, salt, sugar, and vinegar, and is then stuffed into casings and left to cure for several hours or overnight. The longganisa is then fried until crispy and browned, and is usually served with garlic fried rice and eggs for breakfast.
