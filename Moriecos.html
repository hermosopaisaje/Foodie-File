Moriecos

This rice cake (kakanin in Tagalog) is stuffed with latik or caramelized young coconut, steamed and wrapped in fresh banana leaf. Moriecos is a popular snack or dessert in Isabela, and is often sold in local markets and food stalls. It is a simple yet delicious treat that is perfect for satisfying sweet cravings. If you have the chance to visit Isabela, trying Moriecos is a must-try to experience one of the province's unique local delicacies.
