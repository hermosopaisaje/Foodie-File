LusciousDelicacies in Isabela

Isabela Province is where the biggest supply of rice in Northern Luzon comes from. It’s also the biggest supplier of corn in the country, earning its titles “Rice Granary of Northern Luzon” and “Corn Capital of the Philippines.” It’s in the Cagayan Valley region and in the middle of Cagayan Province, Kalinga, Mountain Province, Ifugao, Nueva Vizcaya, Quirino, Aurora the Philippine Sea.



AboutUs

If you love food, we have something for you. We believe that food is not just something we eat to survive, but it's an experience that brings joy and happiness to our lives. We want to share our perspective with you and help you discover new flavors and cuisines. We believe that food is a universal language that brings people together, and we want to use that language to connect with you. So, if you're passionate about food, join us on this journey and let's explore the world of flavors together.
